Overview

The objective of the M.Sc Psychology distance learning program is to provide students with the advanced training in research methods and professional skill. Master of Science in Psychology distance learning course will prepare them both for a career involving psychological research as well as for the application of scientific knowledge and methods in various professional settings. Through the distance Master's in Psychology, learners will gain research skills, clinical reasoning, soft skills, etc. Students will also be able to analyse different human personalities with varied characteristics.

FAQs

What will be the medium of instruction for all programs?
The medium of instruction is English. Hence, candidates are requested to have good English Communication and Comprehension skills.

Are all the programs available in the online format?
The Institution plans to launch all the programs in online format i.e. the student can access the course content online. However, students may have to appear for the exams at the designated exam centers.

Can I take these programs at my own pace or are there any fixed timelines?
You can learn anytime, but the end term examinations and internal assessments should be completed as per the deadlines set by the University.

How do I get to know the detailed syllabus of the program?
It is available in the learning management system (LMS). You can get the subject details of the programs under Academics section.

What is the examination pattern?
Jain (Deemed-to-be University) programs include continuous assessments, home assignments, practical and term exams that include multiple choice and subjective questions.
