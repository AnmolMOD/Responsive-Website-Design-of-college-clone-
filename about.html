About JAIN (Deemed-to-be University)

Established to provide quality education and entrepreneurial development, JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) is an intellectual destination that draws inspired students from more than 38 countries to India's Silicon Valley - Bengaluru. Spread across five campuses, the University has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in India by the India Today Nielsen Best Universities Survey. It has also featured in the QS World Rankings 2018 and has been ranked 351 - 400 among 9000 institutions in the BRICS component. Over the last nine years, the University has made conscious and concerted efforts to build on its strengths and consolidate its achievements. From academic discoveries to athletic records, from artistic creations to scientific breakthroughs, students define JAIN (Deemed-to-be University)'s success in many different ways. JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in distance learning mode, which are flexible and tailor-made to meet the industry's needs. The purpose is to combine open, high-quality courses and advance career opportunities for our students. The learning outcome amalgamates professional associations with industrial bodies and well-established academic content. This ensures that the student requirements and employers' needs are met simultaneously.
