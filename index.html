JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) ODL Programs

JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) has taken an initiative to launch undergraduate and postgraduate programs in distance learning mode. As the digital world continues to expand and e-learning has reached new horizons, the distance learning system becomes equal in educational value to our traditional classes. Our ODL programs offer a self-paced, open schedule, computer-based, and applied learning system. The mobile learning application delivers a modern approach to 'learning on the go' by allowing students to take control over their time and space, where they can access the academic syllabus remotely and on a schedule that fits their needs. JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) has evolved magnificently and gained credibility for quality education and delivery over the years. The open and distance learning (ODL) programs will reflect our reputation, advanced technology, and pedagogy of the highest standards that will assist each aspiring student in attaining higher education and career choices.

JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) Launches Apprenticeship Linked Degree Programs

Distance education is diversifying and expanding at an astounding rate and JAIN (Deemed-to-be University), Center for Distance Education and Virtual Learning (CDEVL) helps you build a robust academic foundation and makes you feel a sense of place in the ever evolving global career launch space. As core competency is taking the edge, our accelerated industry-led distance education programs supported by professional experts and academics, from diverse backgrounds, help reimagine professional skills and deliver smart and informed decisions as effective global leaders.

As a part of this endeavor, JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) has taken the initiative to launch apprenticeship linked degree programs that are equal in educational value to our traditional classes
